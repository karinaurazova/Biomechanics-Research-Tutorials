# Design-space error maps


A global test score can hide localized failure.  The tutorial therefore evaluates error over a grid in fiber fraction and porosity.  This shows where the model is accurate and where it is extrapolating toward poorly sampled or mechanically sensitive regimes.

In a real tissue study such maps can guide new simulations or experiments.  If the surrogate is uncertain in high-porosity low-connectivity regions, one can add RVE simulations there instead of increasing the dataset uniformly.

This is also a reminder that extrapolation is not just a mathematical issue.  It may correspond to tissue states outside the imaging distribution or outside the validity range of the constitutive assumptions.
