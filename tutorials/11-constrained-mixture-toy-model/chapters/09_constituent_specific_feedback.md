# Constituent-specific mechanobiological feedback

Different constituents can respond to different cues: stretch, stress, strain energy, shear, damage, chemical signals, or combinations thereof. The code assigns different transparent hypotheses to the primary family, collagen-like families, and matrix.

These assignments are examples, not biological truths. A useful model exposes the stimulus, target, dead zone, saturation, delay, and sign for every constituent.

Comparing alternative feedback laws is more informative than fitting one highly flexible law to a single endpoint.
