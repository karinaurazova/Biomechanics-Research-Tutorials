# Limitations and connection to later tutorials

The generators do not yet simulate modality-specific optics, electron interaction, staining, polarization transfer, DIC speckles, or neural segmentation. They also do not solve mechanical equilibrium of the generated networks.

These omissions define the next modules. Tutorial 14 will create synthetic microscopy and SEM-like observations; Tutorial 15 will generate polarization-like maps; Tutorial 16 will generate and recover DIC fields; Tutorial 17 will compare classical segmentation, SAM-like prompting, and microscopy-oriented segmentation; Tutorials 18–20 will connect architecture to distributions, constitutive parameters, and multimodal benchmarks.

The central design rule remains unchanged: preserve the latent structure, apply one documented observation model at a time, and never confuse a plausible image with known physical truth.
