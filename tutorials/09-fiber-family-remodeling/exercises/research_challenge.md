# Research challenge

Extend the planar model to a three-dimensional orientation distribution on the unit sphere. Preserve antipodal symmetry, normalization, and non-negativity. Couple deposition direction and survival to family-specific stress, and compare a hereditary cohort implementation with a homogenized internal-variable approximation. Propose a validation dataset that combines biaxial mechanics, unloaded/loaded orientation imaging, collagen content, and turnover markers.
