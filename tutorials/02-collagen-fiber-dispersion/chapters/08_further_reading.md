# 8. Further reading

A useful reading sequence is:

1. **Lanir (1983)** for a foundational structural constitutive framework;
2. **Sacks (2003)** for direct incorporation of experimentally derived planar orientation data;
3. **Gasser, Ogden, and Holzapfel (2006)** for a generalized structure-tensor treatment of dispersed arterial collagen;
4. **Driessen et al. (2008)** for remodeling of angular collagen distributions;
5. **Holzapfel et al. (2015)** for non-symmetric and in-plane/out-of-plane dispersion.

While reading, record for each paper:

- dimensionality of the distribution;
- whether the method uses angular integration or a generalized structure tensor;
- distribution family and normalization;
- treatment of fibers in compression;
- source of structural parameters;
- validation data and loading modes.
