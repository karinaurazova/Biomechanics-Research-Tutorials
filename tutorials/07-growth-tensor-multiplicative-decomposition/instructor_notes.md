# Instructor notes — Tutorial 07

The central teaching risk is presenting `F = Fe Fg` as a unique matrix factorization. Repeatedly emphasize that `Fg` is an internal model state, the intermediate configuration may be incompatible, and additional constitutive assumptions are required.

Recommended checkpoints:

- learners can identify the configuration of every tensor;
- `J = Je Jg` is verified numerically;
- free and constrained growth are distinguished;
- stress is differentiated from the stated reference-volume energy;
- anisotropic increments are composed in chronological order;
- the incompatibility diagnostic is not presented as a complete geometric theorem;
- synthetic verification is not described as tissue validation.
