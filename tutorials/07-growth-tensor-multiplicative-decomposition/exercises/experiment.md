# Experiment

Design a controlled numerical experiment that compares isotropic, diagonal, and full symmetric Mandel-stress-driven growth under the same biaxial total deformation.

Report:

- the complete parameter table;
- time histories of `Fg`, `Fe`, energy, and Mandel stress;
- determinant error `J - Je Jg`;
- a time-step refinement study;
- whether any growth bound becomes active;
- a mechanistic interpretation that does not claim biological validation.
