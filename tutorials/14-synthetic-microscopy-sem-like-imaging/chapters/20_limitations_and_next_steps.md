# Limitations and transition to segmentation

The simulators omit many instrument-specific effects: vectorial diffraction, polarization mixing, realistic fluorophore chemistry, multiple scattering, detector electronics, electron interaction volumes, specimen preparation, beam damage, and charging dynamics. They should be used to verify algorithms and expose assumptions. Tutorial 15 will add polarization-like maps; later modules will use these data for DIC, SAM/μSAM, orientation statistics, and inverse mechanics.
