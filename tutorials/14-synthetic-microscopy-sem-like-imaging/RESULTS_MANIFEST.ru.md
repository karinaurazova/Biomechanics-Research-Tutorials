# Манифест результатов

Все результаты синтетические и воспроизводимые. Static figures are generated by `experiments/scenarios.py`; animations are generated from the same ground-truth arrays. The SEM-like renderer is explicitly phenomenological and must not be interpreted as a CASINO-equivalent electron-transport simulation.

- `figures/`: 22 English/Russian figure pairs.
- `animations/`: focus sweep and FIB-SEM serial-section animations in both languages.
- `data/example_multimodal_dataset.npz`: synchronized ground truth and virtual modalities.
- `data/synthetic_microscopy_benchmark.csv`: verification thresholds and pass/fail values.
