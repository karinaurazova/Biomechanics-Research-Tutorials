# Exercise 3 — Research challenge: toward finite-strain growth

Design a plan for replacing the linearized model with a finite-strain formulation.

Your plan should specify:

- the deformation gradient and growth tensor variables;
- the strain-energy function;
- the residual vector;
- the tangent matrix or numerical differentiation strategy;
- the treatment of incompressibility;
- the verification tests you would keep from this tutorial.

The goal is not to implement everything immediately.  The goal is to map the conceptual bridge from this transparent model to a research-grade solver.
