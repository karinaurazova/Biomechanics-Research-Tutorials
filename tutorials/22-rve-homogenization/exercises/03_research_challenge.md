# Research challenge

Implement one extension:

1. **Affine boundary condition comparison.** Add a kinematic uniform-displacement solution and compare it with the periodic solution.
2. **Uncertainty propagation.** Add noise to the orientation field and compute confidence intervals for `C11`, `C22` and anisotropy ratio.
3. **Structure-to-stiffness surrogate.** Generate many RVEs and train a small regression model that predicts effective stiffness from structural descriptors.
4. **Growth update.** Couple Tutorial 21 and Tutorial 22 by updating the RVE structure after a synthetic growth step.

Write a short note explaining which validation metric would convince you that the extension works.
