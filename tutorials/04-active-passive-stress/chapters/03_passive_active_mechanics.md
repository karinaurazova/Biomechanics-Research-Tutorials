# Passive and active mechanics\n\nFor a transparent baseline, the passive uniaxial response is the incompressible Neo-Hookean nominal stress\n\n\[P_p(\lambda)=\mu(\lambda-\lambda^{-2}).\]\n\nAn active contribution depends on activation and fiber state. The total response is not a new passive material property: it is a superposition or coupling of passive resistance and internally generated contraction. The tutorial keeps the two terms available separately so that plots do not hide their origin.
