# Research Challenge

Create a synthetic multi-mode identification problem.

1. Generate noisy uniaxial and equibiaxial data from one hidden model.
2. Fit at least two alternative model families.
3. Reserve shear data for validation.
4. Quantify parameter uncertainty or correlation.
5. Determine whether the correct model family can be recovered.
6. Discuss non-uniqueness, extrapolation, and experimental design.

An advanced extension is to derive the full first Piola stress tensor by automatic differentiation and compare it with the path-derivative implementation.
