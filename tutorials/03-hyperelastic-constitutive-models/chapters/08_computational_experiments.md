# 08 — Computational experiments

The experiment suite is designed as a sequence of questions.

1. **Model catalog:** What qualitative curve does each of the sixteen models produce?
2. **Isotropic comparison:** Which laws remain similar over moderate uniaxial stretch?
3. **Loading modes:** Do those similarities persist in biaxial loading, plane strain, and shear?
4. **Limiting chains:** How do \(J_m\) and the Arruda–Boyce chain parameter change stiffening?
5. **Ogden exponents:** How does a principal-stretch power alter tension and compression?
6. **Calibration non-uniqueness:** Can two parameter sets fit a narrow calibration interval yet extrapolate differently?
7. **Volumetric penalties:** How do alternative functions penalize compression and dilation?
8. **Fiber angle:** How does alignment with the loading axis affect recruitment?
9. **Fiber-angle map:** Where in stretch–angle space is the response largest?
10. **Dispersion:** How does \(\kappa\) change the GOH response relative to HGO?
11. **Myocardial shear:** Which material planes are predicted to be stiffest?
12. **Derivative verification:** How does finite-difference error depend on step size?
13. **Objectivity:** Does a superposed rigid rotation leave the energy unchanged?
14. **Animation:** How does the Gent locking parameter move the apparent stiffening threshold?

Every saved result has an English and Russian version generated from identical numerical data.
