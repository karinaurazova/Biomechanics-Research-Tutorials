# Tutorial 12 masterclass — Residual stress and ring opening

**Duration:** 210 minutes

## Learning arc
1. Terminology and configurations — 20 min
2. Open-sector derivation — 35 min
3. Numerical equilibrium and verification — 35 min
4. Stress homogenization, axial prestretch, anisotropy — 35 min
5. Multilayer and non-tubular release — 30 min
6. Inverse problem and uncertainty — 35 min
7. Research challenge and discussion — 20 min

## Instructor checkpoints
- Ask which configuration every radius belongs to.
- Require a boundary-condition statement before interpreting an opening shape.
- Compare a geometric observation with a reconstructed stress field.
- Make learners identify at least two parameter-compensation mechanisms.
