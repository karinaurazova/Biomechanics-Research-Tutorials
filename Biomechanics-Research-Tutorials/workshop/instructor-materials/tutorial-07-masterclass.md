# Master class — Tutorial 07: Growth Tensor and Multiplicative Decomposition

**Duration:** 210 minutes. **Format:** derivation, live coding, verification lab, and research-design discussion.

## Learning arc

1. Why elasticity alone cannot represent persistent natural-state change — 15 min
2. Three configurations and `F = Fe Fg` — 25 min
3. Isotropic, transverse, and orthotropic growth tensors — 20 min
4. Energy convention and stress transformation — 25 min
5. Verification: determinants, frame indifference, finite differences — 25 min
6. Homogeneous stress-driven growth — 30 min
7. Noncommutative anisotropic paths — 20 min
8. Incompatibility and residual stress — 20 min
9. Differential-growth strip — 20 min
10. Identifiability and research challenge — 10 min

## Required group deliverables

Each group submits:

- one verified multiplicative decomposition;
- one stress-derivative check;
- one comparison of free and constrained growth;
- one path-dependence result;
- one paragraph explaining why measured `F` does not identify `Fg`;
- a clear separation of verification, validation, and biological interpretation.
