# Tutorial 13 Master Class — Synthetic Fibrous-Tissue Generation

**Duration:** 210 minutes

## Sequence

1. **20 min — Why latent truth matters.** Separate geometry, observation, and mechanics.
2. **30 min — Axial statistics and reproducible randomness.** Demonstrate seeds and concentration.
3. **40 min — Generator families.** Bundles, Mikado, crossing, layered, gradient, and Voronoi networks.
4. **35 min — Raster truth.** Image, mask, orientation, coherence, count, family, and thickness.
5. **30 min — Topology.** Skeletons, endpoints, branches, components, and failure cases.
6. **25 min — Three-dimensional volumes and anisotropic voxels.**
7. **20 min — Verification benchmark and dataset export.**
8. **10 min — Research challenge briefing.**

## Live checkpoints

- Reproduce the same realization from the same seed.
- Create a crossing for which single-axis coherence is low.
- Find two networks with similar porosity and different connectivity.
- Export an NPZ file and inspect its JSON metadata.
