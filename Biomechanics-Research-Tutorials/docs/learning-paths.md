# Learning paths

## Foundations: Tutorials 01–05

Orientation adaptation, dispersion, constitutive mechanics, active response, and image-based orientation extraction.

## Growth and remodeling: Tutorials 06–12

1. Mechanical homeostasis and stability.
2. Finite-growth kinematics and incompatibility.
3. Stress-driven volumetric growth.
4. Fiber-family rotation, distributions, and turnover.
5. ECM production, degradation, maturation, and cross-links.
6. Constrained-mixture history and constituent mechanics.
7. Residual stress, cut release, and inverse reference configurations.

This sequence moves from a scalar feedback loop to tensor growth, constituent histories, and experimentally observable release geometry.

## Imaging-informed and experimental mechanics: Tutorials 05 and 13–20

Tutorial 05 provides structure-tensor foundations. Tutorials 13–16 now provide known-ground-truth tissue generation, microscopy forward models, polarization-like orientation recovery, and synthetic DIC. Tutorials 17–20 will add segmentation, directional statistics, image-informed constitutive parameters, and a multimodal verification benchmark.

## Advanced computation: Tutorials 21–25

Finite elements, representative-volume homogenization, surrogate models, physics-informed learning, and uncertainty analysis.

## Tissue-oriented routes

- **Vessels and tubular organs:** 02 → 03 → 06 → 08 → 09 → 11 → 12.
- **Muscle and active tissues:** 01 → 03 → 04 → 06 → 07 → 11.
- **Tendon, ligament, skin, and fibrous sheets:** 02 → 03 → 09 → 10 → 11 → 12.
- **Imaging workflows:** 05 → 13 → 14 → 15 → 16 → 17–20.
