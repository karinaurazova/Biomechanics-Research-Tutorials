# Teaching guide

Each tutorial can be used in three formats.

## Self-study

The learner reads the chapter sequence, runs the baseline experiment, and completes the Explore tasks.

## Two-hour master class

Suggested structure:

- 20 min: physical and biological motivation;
- 25 min: mathematical model;
- 25 min: guided code walkthrough;
- 30 min: parameter experiment;
- 15 min: interpretation and limitations;
- 5 min: research challenge.

## Project-based course

Students reproduce the baseline, extend one assumption, document verification, and present a short research note.

## Assessment principle

Assessment should reward interpretation, explicit assumptions, and reproducibility—not only the production of a visually attractive plot.
