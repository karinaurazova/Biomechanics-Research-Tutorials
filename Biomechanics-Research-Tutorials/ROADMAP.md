# Roadmap

This roadmap describes the complete 25-module educational sequence.  The tutorials are ordered so that a learner can move from local fibre mechanics to growth, image-informed verification, finite elements, homogenization, surrogate models, physics-informed learning and uncertainty quantification.

| Tutorials | Block | Count | Role in the sequence |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| 01–05 | Foundations | 5 | basic kinematics, stress models, orientation analysis and structure tensors |
| 06–12 | Growth and remodeling | 7 | homeostasis, growth tensors, stress-driven adaptation, ECM turnover, mixtures and residual stress |
| 13–20 | Image-informed and experimental mechanics | 8 | synthetic microstructure, imaging modalities, DIC, segmentation, ODFs, image-informed parameters and multimodal verification |
| 21–25 | Advanced computational methods | 5 | FE growth, RVE homogenization, surrogate modeling, physics-informed learning and uncertainty quantification |

## Final block: advanced computational methods

- **21 — Finite-Element Growth Model:** how incompatible growth creates elastic accommodation and residual stress.
- **22 — RVE Homogenization:** how a heterogeneous microstructure becomes an effective stiffness tensor.
- **23 — Surrogate Modeling:** how expensive RVE/FE simulations can be approximated and audited.
- **24 — Physics-Informed Learning:** how sparse observations, boundary conditions and equilibrium residuals can be combined.
- **25 — Sensitivity Analysis and Uncertainty Quantification:** how uncertain inputs propagate to mechanical predictions and reliability.
