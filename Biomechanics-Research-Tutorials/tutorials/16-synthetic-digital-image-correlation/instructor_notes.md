# Instructor notes

A 180–210 minute class can be organized around four blocks: image formation and texture; subset matching and subpixel refinement; displacement-to-strain processing; verification and failure modes. Require students to inspect a correlation surface before using a field plot. Emphasize that subset size and step size have different meanings. The crack example should be used to discuss model inadequacy rather than merely low correlation.

All numerical values are synthetic. Do not present the benchmark as experimental validation.
