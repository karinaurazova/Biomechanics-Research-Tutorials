# Exact synthetic displacement fields

Verification requires fields with known analytical behavior. The tutorial includes rigid translation, uniaxial and biaxial deformation, simple shear, rigid rotation, bending-like motion, smooth heterogeneity, and localized deformation. These cases probe different algorithmic properties.

Rigid motion tests sign conventions and objectivity. Affine fields test subset shape functions and strain differentiation. Smooth non-affine fields test spatial resolution. Localization and discontinuities reveal averaging and failure near cracks, folds, or sliding interfaces.
