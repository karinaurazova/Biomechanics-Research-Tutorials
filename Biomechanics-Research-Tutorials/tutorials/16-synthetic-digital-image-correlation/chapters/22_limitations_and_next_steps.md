# Limitations and next steps

The implementation is a didactic local 2D solver. It does not include a production multiscale pyramid, robust global regularization, stereo calibration, lens-distortion correction, temporal tracking, GPU acceleration, discontinuous enrichment, or digital volume correlation.

Its purpose is to make assumptions visible and to create known-ground-truth data. Tutorial 17 will address microstructure segmentation with classical methods, SAM, and μSAM. Later modules will combine image structure, DIC kinematics, forces, and constitutive models in inverse identification and multimodal verification.
