# Instructor notes

Recommended duration: 210 minutes.

1. Definitions and configuration diagram — 25 min.
2. Open-sector derivation and coding — 45 min.
3. Loaded equilibrium and verification — 35 min.
4. Multilayer, axial, and anisotropic effects — 35 min.
5. Strip release and growth incompatibility — 25 min.
6. Inverse problem, uncertainty, and experimental design — 30 min.
7. Discussion and research challenge — 15 min.

Emphasize that opening angle is an observation, whereas stress is a reconstructed field. Ask learners to state the configuration of every tensor and the boundary conditions of every release protocol.
