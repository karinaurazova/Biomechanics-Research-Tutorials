# Exercises

## Explore
1. Sweep opening angle and identify the angle minimizing loaded stress variation for three pressures.
2. Change axial prestretch and explain the incompressibility coupling.
3. Compare isotropic and fiber-reinforced walls at fixed released geometry.

## Experiment
1. Replace neo-Hookean response by a Mooney–Rivlin law and repeat the inverse sweep.
2. Create a two-layer model with different opening angles and compare whole-wall and separated-layer release.
3. Propagate uncertainty in angle, wall thickness, modulus, and axial stretch.

## Research challenge
Design a minimal set of cuts and mechanical measurements that can distinguish opening-angle uncertainty from constitutive-model uncertainty. State what remains structurally non-identifiable.
