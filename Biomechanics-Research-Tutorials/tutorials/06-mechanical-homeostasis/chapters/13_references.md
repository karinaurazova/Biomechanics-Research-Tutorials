# 13 — References

The repository includes machine-readable entries in [`references.bib`](../references.bib).

Core sources include:

- Rodriguez, Hoger, and McCulloch (1994) for stress-dependent finite growth kinematics;
- Humphrey and Rajagopal (2002) for constrained-mixture growth and remodeling;
- Alford, Humphrey, and Taber (2008) for arterial growth and remodeling;
- Cyron, Wilson, and Humphrey (2014) for mechanobiological stability;
- Cyron, Aydin, and Humphrey (2016) for a homogenized constrained-mixture model;
- Latorre and Humphrey (2018) for mechanobiologically equilibrated constrained mixtures;
- Irons, Estrada, and Humphrey (2022) for signaling control of aortic mechanical homeostasis.

The equations implemented here are reduced educational constructions. Citations provide conceptual and methodological context; they do not imply that the tutorial reproduces every equation or parameter of the cited work.
