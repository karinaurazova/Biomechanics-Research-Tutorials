# Explore

1. Change the random seed while keeping all other parameters fixed. Which metrics remain stable and which vary strongly?
2. Increase axial concentration from 0 to 30. Compare global order, local coherence, and the appearance of the image.
3. Change radius at fixed fiber count. Explain why porosity, apparent thickness, and connected components change together.
4. Create two crossing families with separations of 15°, 45°, and 90°. Where does a single-axis orientation become unreliable?
5. Compare a Mikado network and a Voronoi-like network at similar area fraction.
