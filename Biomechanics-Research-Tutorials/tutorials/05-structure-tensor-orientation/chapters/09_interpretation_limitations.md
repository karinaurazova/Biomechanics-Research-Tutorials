# 09 Interpretation and limitations

The output is an orientation estimate of image texture at chosen scales. It is not automatically a collagen direction, cardiomyocyte direction, material symmetry axis, or constitutive parameter. That interpretation requires an imaging-specific validation chain.

For research use, document preprocessing, spatial calibration, staining or contrast mechanism, expected number of families, uncertainty, and independent comparison where possible. This tutorial intentionally stops at a verification-ready synthetic benchmark.
