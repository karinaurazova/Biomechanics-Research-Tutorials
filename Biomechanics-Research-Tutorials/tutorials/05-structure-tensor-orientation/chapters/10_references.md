# 10 References

Primary and application references are listed in [`references.bib`](../references.bib). The methodological starting point is the least-squares orientation detector of Bigun and Granlund. Biomedical applications and validation studies are included to show how scale, anisotropy, and ground truth are handled in practice.

The equations in this tutorial are implemented independently and verified on synthetic data. No published experimental dataset is redistributed.
