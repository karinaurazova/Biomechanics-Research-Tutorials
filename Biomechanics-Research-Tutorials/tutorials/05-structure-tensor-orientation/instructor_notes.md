# Instructor notes

Recommended duration: 150 minutes.

- 0–20 min: image orientation versus material orientation;
- 20–45 min: tensor derivation and axial angles;
- 45–70 min: notebook pipeline;
- 70–95 min: noise and scale study;
- 95–120 min: boundaries, illumination, and confidence masks;
- 120–140 min: crossing-fiber failure;
- 140–150 min: interpretation checklist.

Do not present coherence as a probability. Require every group to report coverage with error and to state whether one local direction is a plausible assumption.
