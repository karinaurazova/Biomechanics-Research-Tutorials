# Explore

1. Change isotropic linear growth from 0.8 to 1.3 and verify that `det(Fg)` equals the cube of the linear factor.
2. Compare free growth `F = Fg` with constrained growth `F = I` for the same tensor.
3. Rotate an orthotropic growth tensor and verify that its principal growth stretches do not change.
4. Set the growth-law rate to zero and confirm that the internal state remains fixed.
5. Increase the bulk modulus and inspect the elastic volume ratio during constrained growth.
