# Instructor notes

Recommended duration: 210 minutes.

The central teaching risk is allowing learners to interpret a decreasing scalar error as restoration of the full stress state. Require them to plot both mean and von Mises stress. A second risk is mixing pressure and tensile mean-stress signs. Write the convention on the board before any simulation.

Suggested sequence:

1. kinematics and free/constrained growth;
2. stress-measure comparison;
3. response law and homeostatic band;
4. fixed-deformation versus fixed-stress control;
5. hydrostatic/deviatoric limitation;
6. mass-density bookkeeping;
7. numerical positivity and stability;
8. spatial regularization and identifiability.

Do not present the synthetic parameter values as physiological ranges.
