# Experiment

Design a reproducible comparison of three constitutive choices:

- mean-Mandel-driven growth;
- mean-Cauchy-driven growth;
- energy-driven growth.

Use step, pulse and cyclic loading. Report the target, normalization scale, boundary control, time step, rate bounds, final \(J_g\), residual stimulus and whether any numerical bound was activated. Do not rank models from one loading protocol alone.
