# Active stress\n\nThe current fiber direction is\n\n\[\mathbf a=\frac{\mathbf F\mathbf a_0}{\|\mathbf F\mathbf a_0\|}.\]\n\nThe simplest active Cauchy stress is\n\n\[\boldsymbol\sigma_a=T_a\,\mathbf a\otimes\mathbf a.\]\n\nThe first Piola stress follows from\n\n\[\mathbf P_a=J\boldsymbol\sigma_a\mathbf F^{-T}.\]\n\nThis construction is easy to interpret and add to a passive stress. It also requires explicit choices about current direction, transverse active components, stress measure, and the dependence of `T_a` on deformation.
