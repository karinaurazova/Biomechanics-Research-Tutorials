# Motivation

Active soft tissues generate internal force while simultaneously resisting deformation through passive material structure. Cardiac muscle, skeletal muscle, and engineered contractile tissues therefore cannot be described by a passive constitutive law alone. A useful model must say **what activates**, **how activation changes force**, and **how that force enters continuum mechanics**.

This tutorial uses small synthetic models to make these choices visible. It does not claim that one formulation is universally correct. Instead, it asks which experiments can distinguish the formulations.
