# Activation, force–length, and force–velocity\n\nThe educational active tension is\n\n\[T_a=T_{\max}A(t)f_L(\lambda_f)f_V(v_s).\]\n\n`A(t)` is a normalized time signal. `f_L` peaks near an optimal fiber stretch and represents overlap-dependent force. `f_V` decreases during rapid shortening and increases to a bounded level during active lengthening. Each factor is dimensionless; `T_max` sets the stress scale.\n\nThe functions are separated in code so that their assumptions can be tested independently.
