# Computational protocols

The module uses: (1) fixed-length isometric twitches; (2) quasi-static afterload balance for isotonic shortening; (3) one-point calibration between active stress and active strain; (4) simple shear after calibration; (5) parameter maps over stretch and activation.

Each experiment answers a different question. Isometric data constrain time and length dependence. Afterload protocols show shortening capacity. Shear reveals constitutive coupling that a uniaxial test cannot identify.
