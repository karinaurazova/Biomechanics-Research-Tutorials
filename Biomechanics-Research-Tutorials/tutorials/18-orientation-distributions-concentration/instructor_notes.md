# Instructor notes — Tutorial 18

Recommended pacing: 60–90 minutes.

1. Begin with the difference between directional and axial data.
2. Show the modality overview and ask students which representation they trust most.
3. Compute ODFs and concentration parameters.
4. Discuss why a visually small segmentation error can produce a large change in downstream stiffness.
5. End by previewing Tutorial 19, where these quantities become inputs to constitutive parameter identification.
