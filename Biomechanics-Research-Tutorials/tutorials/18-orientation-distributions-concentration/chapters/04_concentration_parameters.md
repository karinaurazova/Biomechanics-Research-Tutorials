# Concentration parameters

The tutorial reports the axial mean direction, resultant length, von-Mises concentration parameter `kappa`, orientation tensor and entropy. These quantities are not interchangeable. The mean direction describes the dominant axis, while concentration describes how strongly orientations cluster around it.

For biomechanics, concentration controls how much of the material response is directional rather than isotropic. A small error in concentration can therefore change the predicted stiffness even if the image visually appears similar.
