# Explore

## E1 — Normalize the density

For $\mu=20^\circ$ and $\beta=4$, compute the numerical integral of $\rho$ using 33, 65, 129, and 513 points. Report the absolute normalization error.

## E2 — Interpret concentration

Plot $\beta=0$, 0.5, 2, 8, and 20. For each case, report $S$ and $D=1-S$. Explain the limiting cases.

## E3 — Verify the tensor

Compare `orientation_tensor` and `numerical_orientation_tensor` for three mean angles and three concentration values. Report the largest component error.

## E4 — Reference state

Confirm numerically that energy and nominal stress vanish at $\lambda=1$. Explain which modeling choices make this possible.
